# co-op.care — Care Tier Strategy
## From First Hour to Full Ownership

**Date:** March 10, 2026
**Purpose:** Define the engagement-to-equity pipeline that transforms casual neighbors into committed cooperative owners through three tiers of deepening care involvement.

---

## The Insight

Every cooperative faces the same challenge: how do you move someone from "I signed up" to "I'm an owner who votes, governs, and shares in the surplus"?

Most co-ops use flat membership dues. Pay $100, you're a member. But that doesn't create **progressive commitment** — the feeling that your relationship with the community deepens as you give more.

co-op.care uses **Care Hours** — actual time spent helping neighbors — as the natural progression currency. The more you give, the more the community gives back.

---

## Three Tiers of Care

### 🌱 Seedling — *Planting Your First Roots* (0–39 Care Hours/year)

You just joined. You helped a neighbor with groceries. You drove someone to a doctor's appointment. Every hour counts.

**What you get:**
- 1.0× earning rate on all Care Hours
- Standard matching (4-hour SLA)
- 5 bonus hours per referral (both of you)
- 3 Sage AI sessions per month
- CII burnout assessment access
- Community newsletter and updates
- Basic orientation training
- Credit expires after 12 months

**The feeling:** "I'm part of something. My hours matter."

You've been showing up. Week after week, you've helped neighbors with meals, companionship, rides, tech support. Forty hours means you've averaged almost an hour a week for the past year.

## How It Works

### Earning Care Hours
Every completed Time Bank task earns Care Hours. Drive someone to the pharmacy? Earn hours. Share a meal with an isolated senior? Earn hours. Help someone set up their phone? Earn hours.

Your tier's multiplier applies to every hour:
- Seedling: 1 hour of care = 1.0 Care Hour
- Rooted: 1 hour of care = 1.25 Care Hours
- Canopy: 1 hour of care = 1.5 Care Hours

### Rolling 12-Month Window
Your tier is calculated from Care Hours in the last 12 months. This isn't "set and forget" — it rewards sustained commitment. If you stop showing up, you'll naturally move back down.

**But we never punish you.** Credits earned at a higher tier keep their value. Tier changes happen quarterly (not immediately), and you'll always get a heads-up: "You're 5 hours away from maintaining Rooted status."

### The Path to Equity

| Stage | Tier | What Happens |
|-------|------|-------------|
| **Learn** | 🌱 Seedling | Understand the cooperative model. See how ownership works. |
| **Earn** | 🌳 Rooted | You've proven commitment. Purchase equity shares ($100 min). |
| **Own** | 🏔️ Canopy | Full patronage dividends. Vote + propose. Run for the Board. |

This is the pipeline: **Care → Recognition → Ownership → Governance**

## How Tiers Relate to Everything Else

| System | Relationship |
|--------|-------------|
| **Membership ($100/$200)** | Financial buy-in. Separate from tier. You need membership AND Rooted tier for equity eligibility. |
| **Streaks (weekly consistency)** | Streaks reward showing up every week. Tiers reward total annual volume. They stack — a Canopy member with a 52-week streak is legendary. |
| **Comfort Card Plans (Free/$59/$149)** | Service level. Any tier can have any plan. A Seedling can subscribe to Shield. |
| **5-in-30 Catalyst Challenge** | The on-ramp. Challenge gets you to 5 hours → tier system keeps you going to 40, then 120. |
| **Referral Bonuses** | Increase with tier: 5→7→10 hours. The more you're invested, the more valuable your invitation becomes. |

## Financial Impact

### Earning Multiplier Economics
- **Seedling** (1.0×): 40 hours of care = 40 Care Hours
- **Rooted** (1.25×): 40 hours of care = 50 Care Hours (25% bonus for loyalty)
- **Canopy** (1.5×): 40 hours of care = 60 Care Hours (50% bonus for deep commitment)

At $35/hr market equivalent, a Canopy member earning 120+ real hours generates 180+ Care Hours worth $6,300+ in community value.

### Referral Amplification
- Seedling: 5 hrs × $35 = $175 per referral
- Rooted: 7 hrs × $35 = $245 per referral (40% more)
- Canopy: 10 hrs × $35 = $350 per referral (100% more)

Higher tiers bring higher-value referrals because they've proven the model works.

### Patronage Dividend Pipeline
At Rooted tier:
- Year 1 equity purchase: $100-$200
- Annual patronage dividend: `(member_hours / total_hours) × net_surplus`
- 20% minimum cash distribution
- 80% retained as equity (5-year linear vesting at 20%/year)
- By Year 5: ~$52,000 in vested equity (based on worker-owner projections)
