# co-op.care — ACO Partnership One-Pager

## The Post-Acute Visibility Gap Is Costing Your ACO Money

Your ACO manages inpatient and outpatient care brilliantly. But what happens in the 95% of time patients spend at home between visits? That's where readmissions start, falls happen, medications get missed, and family caregivers burn out — triggering the most expensive care transitions.

**co-op.care fills this gap with cooperative-model companion care that feeds data back to your care team.**

---

## What We Do

co-op.care is a **worker-owned cooperative** providing long-term companion care for aging adults. Our caregivers are W-2 employees earning $25-28/hr with full benefits and equity ownership. The result: **15% projected turnover vs. the industry's 77%** — meaning your patients get the same caregiver, the same trust, the same relationship month after month.

### Services (Class B Licensed)
- Daily/weekly companion care visits
- Medication adherence monitoring
- Meal preparation and nutrition support
- Transportation to appointments
- Fall prevention and home safety
- Caregiver burnout prevention (validated CII-12 assessment)
- Social isolation reduction

### Technology Platform
- **Real-time care logs** visible to your care team
- **Galaxy Watch RPM** — vitals, activity, sleep, anomaly detection
- **Sage AI companion** — 24/7 support for family caregivers between visits
- **FHIR R4 interoperability** — data flows directly into your EHR via Aidbox

## Integration Model

```
Your ACO Care Team
        ↓ Referral (post-discharge or PCP-identified)
co-op.care Assignment
        ↓ Matched caregiver (proximity + skills + compatibility)
Home Companion Care
        ↓ Daily care logs + Galaxy Watch vitals
Data Back to Your Team
        ↓ FHIR Observations via Aidbox
Informed Clinical Decisions
```
