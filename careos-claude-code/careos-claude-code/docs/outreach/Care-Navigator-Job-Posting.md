## Own What You Build

co-op.care is a worker-owned home care cooperative. We're not an agency that places you — you ARE the agency. As a founding Care Navigator, you'll earn W-2 wages, benefits, and equity in the cooperative from Day 1.

No private equity. No 1099 gig work. No getting flipped.

---

## The Role

Care Navigators are our founding team members — building relationships with Boulder families before our state home care license arrives. You're the bridge between families who need help and a community that wants to show up.

**What you'll do:**

- **Assess families** — Conduct caregiver burnout and patient needs assessments (CII/CRI) alongside our Medical Director
- **Build relationships** — Get to know each family's story, routines, and what matters to them
- **Connect community** — Link families to local resources — meals, transport, support groups, respite
- **Coordinate the Time Bank** — Match neighbors who can help with families who need it
- **Learn the platform** — Train on our CareOS voice-to-text system so you're ready when the license arrives

**What this is NOT (yet):**

- No hands-on personal care until our Class B license is issued (~4-5 months)
- No medical or skilled nursing tasks

The families you've been building relationships with for months? They become your companion care clients. No handoff. No stranger showing up. Same caregiver, same trust.

You'll already know:

- Which neighbor can help with rides to the pharmacy

That knowledge is irreplaceable. That's why you own it.
