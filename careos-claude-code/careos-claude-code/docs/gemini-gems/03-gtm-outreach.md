## TARGET CUSTOMER SEGMENTS

**Segment 1: Alpha Daughter (Primary — B2C)**
Women aged 45–60 managing care for an aging parent. She is overwhelmed by fragmented care options, terrified of nursing homes, and already doing informal caregiving. She wants one trusted relationship that scales with her parent's needs. Conversion: CII assessment → density map → founding deposit.

**Segment 2: Employers (Primary — B2B)**
Companies offering co-op.care membership as an employee benefit. Framed as "Age at Home Care Insurance." Targets HR/benefits leaders whose employees are losing productivity to eldercare responsibilities. This is the primary growth channel.

**Segment 3: PACE Programs (Institutional)**
TRU PACE (Lafayette, CO) as the first sub-capitation partner. co-op.care delivers home-based care under PACE capitation for dual-eligible seniors. Highest-margin revenue layer. November 2026 pitch is Tier 1 milestone.

**Segment 4: Partners**
Community wellness providers (yoga studios, fitness programs, nutrition, senior centers, mental health) who receive referrals from co-op.care and benefit from HSA/FSA eligibility via Medical Director letters.

**Segment 5: Investors**
Angel investors and mission-aligned funds interested in cooperative economics, healthcare innovation, and community-based care models.
