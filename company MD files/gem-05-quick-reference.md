## WHAT WE ARE
Healthcare technology platform powering worker-owned home care cooperatives. Boulder, Colorado. First cooperative with enterprise-grade clinical AI + FHIR R4. Technology vendor to local cooperatives. "National Brain, Local Hands, Zero Extraction."

**Critical distinction:** co-op.care is the LLC (Hard Shell). The cooperative is separate (Soft Center). We don't run cooperatives — we build the OS that makes them healthcare delivery organizations.

## THE PROBLEM
63M unpaid family caregivers. 77% caregiver turnover. Agencies extract 40–60%. Workers get $13–17/hr. Families pay $9,900/month for facility care. The Alpha Daughter (45–60, working, exhausted) carries it all.

## THE SOLUTION
Family pays $30/hr → Caregiver gets $26/hr (W-2 + equity) → Cooperative retains $4/hr for oversight. Same caregiver every week. $52K equity over 5 years. CareOS clinical AI. Time Bank community layer.
